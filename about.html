4DSNexus

The name, explained

4DS Nexus is the platform. 4DS Solutions is the company.

4DS Nexus is the product you experience on this site, a connected platform for CRM, client portals, inventory, reporting, mobile access, and workflow automation. The word nexus means a central connection point, which is what the platform does for your operations.

It is built and supported by 4DS Solutions (Pty) Ltd, a South African technology company. We also create custom websites, e-commerce stores, and client portals with the same care as the platform. 4DS Nexus is a division of 4DS Solutions. You work with a focused product brand backed by an established company.

Our Approach

Practical technology for real businesses

Every feature in 4DS Nexus exists because real businesses need it, not because it sounds good on a brochure. We focus on:

  • Clear delivery: core services today, custom scope when needed
  • Accessibility: pricing and support for businesses of all sizes
