Hosting, domains & third-party costs

Platform hosting for 4DS Nexus is included in your subscription (subject to plan storage limits). For websites, mobile apps, and custom projects:

  • Domain registration and renewal
  • Separate hosting (if not on Nexus)
  • SMS gateways and messaging credits
  • Maps and geolocation API usage
  • Payment gateway fees (PayFast, Ozow, Stripe, etc.)
  • Other third-party subscriptions you choose

These may incur separate charges from providers or be quoted as part of your project. We will always confirm before you commit.

Application hosting may use third-party providers (e.g. Afrihost, HostAfrica, Vercel, or equivalent). Clients may use their own hosting where compatible.

* Secure cloud hosting on Core and bundled plans refers to 4DS Nexus application hosting as part of the service.
